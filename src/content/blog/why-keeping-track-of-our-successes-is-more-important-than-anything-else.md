Just a thought of our past success can increase the serotonin. The more you win, the more you will like to win. It is the momentum that allows us to move forward even if all the odds are against us.

![](../../assets/images/wp/arnold-exconde-289207-1024x665.jpg)

In recent years, life has become more demanding than ever. Life dynamics are changing too fast. It demands greater focus, accountability, and discipline from each of us.

But no matter what we are doing or where we are, just a thought of our past success can increase the serotonin. By [creatively visualizing](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B01MSL5SIL/) the successes repeatedly, we can significantly create "[The Winner Effect](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1250001676/)". The more you win, the more you will like to win. It is a momentum that allows us to move forward even if all the odds are against us.

In general, we sporadically focus on our past successes when we are actively trying to get out of temporary set-backs. Habitually tracking and analyzing past successes may tremendously increase the personal belief.

Here are a few points emphasizing the importance of tracking and visualizing our past successes.

## Internal motivation

Internal motivation helps us get things done meticulously fast. It increases focus and interest. When we think about past successes, our self-doubt goes away and it gives us a momentary high. It increases the focus and instantly boosts the productivity. It may even work better than some medicine.

## Faith in self

It is no secret that self-belief is key to confidence, risk, and achievements. It is the most important factor that decides whether we stand up or not after a fall. Repetitive success visualization can increase the faith in self. It boosts the ever-lasting confidence.

## Amplify our strengths

Analyzing our past successes and self-reflecting on them will illuminate our obvious strengths. It will take our focus away from criticism and magically allow us to dwell upon our strengths. It will filter out all the negative noise and help us strengthen our positive traits. Eventually, our strengths become our habits and they reflect in everything we do.

In summary, success-tracking is the most important step towards our personal growth. It is a miracle remedy to boost the productivity, discipline, and focus. It lessens the fear of failure and increases our tenacity. We strengthen the ability to have challenging goals and achieve them. It is [the compound effect](https://a.co/d/hj5zMjH) that grows by each experience.
