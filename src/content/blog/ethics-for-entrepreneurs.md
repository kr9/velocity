When you're an entrepreneur, sometimes you find yourself in a position where there are ethical issues that you need to address. But more importantly, ethics are critical when it comes to making good business decisions. Here's what you need to know about business ethics that every entrepreneur should be aware of.

## How important is being a good person when you're trying to build a business?

The world of entrepreneurship is a tricky one. You need to be smart and innovative in order to succeed, but you also need to think about the impact that your business will have on the world around you. Ethics is an important part of this puzzle because it can help you make decisions that are right for everyone involved. Ethical entrepreneurship is the art and science of making decisions that maximize the long-term value of corporate assets while preserving the value of stakeholders, including customers, employees, shareholders, and society at large.

## Entrepreneurship is a profession of integrity and honesty

Entrepreneurship is a profession of integrity and honesty. Honesty is the best policy and the most important quality in an entrepreneur. Entrepreneurs are held to a higher standard of honesty than other professions because they have to be very upfront with investors, vendors, and customers about their products or services.

This means that if you want to succeed as an entrepreneur, then you must be honest with yourself first before anyone else can trust your words or actions.

## Integrity includes honesty, trustworthiness, and fairness in all dealings

Integrity means doing what you say you are going to do, and being honest, trustworthy, and fair in all dealings.
